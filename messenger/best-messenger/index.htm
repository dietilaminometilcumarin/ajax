What is a Messenger?

A messenger is a software application designed to facilitate instant communication between people over the internet. Unlike traditional SMS or emails, messengers allow real-time chatting, file sharing, voice and video calls, and much more — all within one convenient platform. They have become essential tools for personal communication, business collaboration, social networking, and entertainment.

Modern messengers go far beyond simple text messaging. They integrate a variety of interactive features that transform the way we connect with others daily.
