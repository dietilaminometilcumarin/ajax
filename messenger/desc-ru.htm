Combined Digital Platform

A multifunctional space for communication, blogging, entertainment, and business.

Platform Overview

A combined digital platform that integrates multiple services for comfortable communication and productivity. This fast and lightweight application allows you to chat, make video calls, send emojis and voice messages, run personal channels, and use a wide range of external services — from weather forecasts to ChatGPT and cryptocurrency managers.

The app is a mini social network equipped with powerful tools for bloggers, friends, and business partners.

Easy Start

Getting started is very simple:

  • launch the app;
  • enter your email address;
  • receive a secret code via email;
  • enter this code into the registration window;
  • click JOIN.

Maximum Communication

The platform consists of interconnected subsystems. As the service evolves, new subsystems will appear, expanding both the primary and additional functionality.

Messenger

The messaging subsystem allows you to:

  • send text, photo, and video messages;
  • make P2P video calls;
  • send voice messages;
  • share emotions and important information.

For Bloggers

Private channels allow bloggers to run both public and private media channels. Private channels can be monetized. Posts may include images, videos, titles, and descriptions. Subscribers can comment and leave likes.

The channel owner's identity remains hidden from users, increasing personal safety.

External Services

Available tools include:

  • online games;
  • bitcoin currency managers;
  • interactive chats (including ChatGPT);
  • worldwide weather services;
  • and much more.

Content Monetization

The built-in Bitcoin wallet allows you to:

  • send donations to other users;
  • pay for premium features;
  • subscribe to paid channels.

For Free People

The application helps solve daily tasks quickly and efficiently, without unnecessary clutter. It is an open platform without censorship or interference, designed for free expression, creativity, and communication.
